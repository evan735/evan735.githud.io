About — The Unheard Emotions

We are a volunteer-run listening platform focused on offering free, compassionate company to people feeling lonely, anxious, or in need of someone to talk to. Our listeners are trained to hold space, not to give professional therapy. If you need clinical support, we will help you find resources.

How it works

  1. Choose a slot in "Book Session".
  2. Fill a short form with what you prefer for the session.
  3. You’ll get a confirmation and a downloadable calendar invite (client-side).
  4. At the scheduled time, connect through the method chosen (phone, video link, or chat).

A note on safety

This service is for emotional support and companionship. It is not a replacement for emergency services or professional mental health care. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services.

Why free?

We want support to be accessible to students and people on tight budgets. The platform runs on volunteer time and community contributions.

Frequently asked questions

Is this professional therapy?
+
No — this is peer emotional support and friendly listening. If you need clinical therapy, we can help you find professional resources.
Is it really free?
+
Yes. Sessions are offered free by volunteers. Donations and community support help keep us running.
What if I'm in crisis?
+
If you're in immediate danger or thinking of harming yourself, please contact local emergency services right away. This platform is not a crisis hotline.
How private are sessions?
+
We ask listeners to respect confidentiality. We do not record sessions without consent. However, do not share highly sensitive legal/medical information here.
